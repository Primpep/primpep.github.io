Legal & Privacy Policy

1. Privacy Policy (GDPR Compliant)

PRIMPEP only collects your contact information for business quotation and order communication. We will never sell, rent or disclose your personal data to any third parties without your written consent. All customer information is stored securely and only used for our cooperation communication.

2. Product Compliance Statement

All skincare raw materials and finished cosmetics supplied by PRIMPEP have passed China NMPA cosmetic filing, meet domestic production standards. We provide complete export documents for customs clearance of each batch of goods, and do not add prohibited ingredients of cosmetics in any country/region.

3. Return & Refund Terms

Bulk raw material orders support sample inspection before mass shipment. If the product quality fails to meet the COA standard, we support free re-delivery or full refund after verification. Custom private label finished goods cannot be returned unless there is a production quality defect.
